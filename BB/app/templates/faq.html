Getting Started

How do I create an account? expand_more
Click Get Started in the top-right corner and fill in your name, email, and password. You'll be signed in instantly. To rent, you'll also need to upload a government ID to your profile.
Do I need to verify my identity? expand_more
Yes. Before making your first booking, you must upload a valid government-issued ID (Aadhaar, Passport, or Driving Licence) from your profile. This keeps everyone safe on the platform.
Is Borrow Box available across India? expand_more
Yes! Borrow Box operates across 30+ cities in India, including Bangalore, Mumbai, Delhi, Hyderabad, and Pune. We're expanding rapidly — if your city isn't covered, list anyway and start a local community!

Renting Items

What is the security deposit? expand_more
A refundable ₹2,000 security deposit is required per active rental. This covers potential damage. If you return the item in good condition, the deposit is fully refunded. You can hold a cumulative balance for multiple rentals.
Can I cancel a booking? expand_more
Pending bookings can be removed from your cart at any time before checkout. Once confirmed, please contact our support team to assist with cancellations. Our cancellation policy applies.
What if the item is damaged during my rental? expand_more
You're responsible for returning items in the condition you received them. Accidental damage during a rental may result in a deduction from your security deposit. Our team mediates fairly in any dispute.
How do I receive a booking confirmation? expand_more
After confirming checkout, both you and the item owner receive a detailed PDF booking confirmation via email, including rental dates, cost breakdown, and terms.

Listing Your Items

Is listing free? expand_more
Absolutely free. There's no charge to list any number of items on Borrow Box. We only earn a small service fee from renters, not owners.
What items can I list? expand_more
Tools, electronics, cameras, sporting gear, musical instruments, fashion, furniture, and more. Items must be safe, legal, and in working condition. Consumables and perishables cannot be listed.
How do I set my rental price? expand_more
You're in full control. Set a daily rental price when listing your item. We suggest pricing at 5–10% of the item's market value per day. You can change the price anytime from your dashboard.

Payments & Refunds

Are payments secure? expand_more
Yes. All transactions are protected with 256-bit SSL encryption. We use industry-standard payment gateways and your card or UPI details are never stored on our servers.
When do I get my deposit back? expand_more
Security deposits are held in your Borrow Box wallet and applied toward future rentals. You can request a withdrawal anytime from your dashboard — processing takes 2–5 business days.
