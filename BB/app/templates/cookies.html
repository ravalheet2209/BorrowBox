What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember your preferences, keep you logged in, and understand how you use the site to improve the experience.

Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies
Always Active

Required for the website to function. These include session cookies to keep you logged in, CSRF tokens for security, and cart state. These cannot be disabled.

Functional Cookies
Optional

Enable enhanced functionality like remembering your search filters, language preferences, and dismissing prompts (e.g., the page loader is only shown once per session).

Analytics Cookies
Optional

Help us understand how users interact with the platform — pages visited, time spent, and errors encountered. Data is anonymized and used only to improve the product.

Marketing Cookies
Not Used

We do not currently use third-party marketing or advertising cookies on Borrow Box. We will update this policy if that changes.
